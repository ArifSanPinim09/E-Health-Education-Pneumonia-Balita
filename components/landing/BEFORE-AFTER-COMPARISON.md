## Key Differences

### Design Language
| Aspect | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Vibe | SaaS Startup | Health Institution |
| Colors | Neon Blue (#2563EB) | Deep Sage (#2F5D50) |
| Typography | Sans-serif only | Serif + Sans |
| Layout | Card-heavy | Editorial |
| Spacing | Uniform py-20 | Varied (24-120px) |
| Shadows | Heavy shadows | Minimal |
| Animations | Many (scale, bounce, loop) | Minimal (fade only) |
| Radius | rounded-2xl, rounded-3xl | rounded-lg max |

### Content Tone
| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| "Mengapa Memilih Program Ini?" | "Kenapa Edukasi Penting" |
| "Bagaimana Program Ini Bekerja?" | "Program 5 Hari" |
| "Siap Melindungi Balita Anda?" | "Mulai Lindungi Balita Anda Hari Ini" |
| Marketing-heavy | Educational-focused |

### Layout Philosophy
| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Grid cards everywhere | Alternating content |
| Horizontal layouts | Vertical timelines |
| Symmetric design | Editorial asymmetry |
| Template-based | Custom institutional |

## Impact

### Before Problems
- ❌ Terlihat seperti template generic
- ❌ Terlalu banyak biru neon
- ❌ Terlalu banyak card dengan shadow
- ❌ Animasi berlebihan
- ❌ Tidak mencerminkan seriusnya topik kesehatan

### After Solutions
- ✅ Unique institutional design
- ✅ Warm color palette yang trustable
- ✅ Clean layout tanpa card overload
- ✅ Animasi subtle dan professional
- ✅ Mencerminkan platform edukasi kesehatan berbasis penelitian

## User Perception

### Before
"Ini seperti landing page SaaS biasa"

### After
"Ini terlihat seperti platform edukasi kesehatan yang serius dan terpercaya"
